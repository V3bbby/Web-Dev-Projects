City Area

  • GracieGrace – Run by Gracie. A high-end boutique selling rare, expensive furniture and clothing. You must pass Gracie’s fashion checks to unlock the full store.
  • Shampoodle – Owned by Harriet. Lets you change your hairstyle and later wear Mii masks (a Wii-exclusive feature).
  • Fortune Shop – Run by Katrina. Offers daily fortunes that can influence luck.
  • Auction House Allows players to sell items to others online asynchronously.
  • Theater Hosts Dr. Shrunk’s comedy shows, where you learn new emotions/reactions.

WII Features

In Animal Crossing: City Folk, the Wii-specific features centered on integrating the console’s hardware and online capabilities into the gameplay experience, most notably through support for Wii Speak voice chat during online visits and the ability to wear Mii masks that displayed your Wii Mii’s face in-game, making it feel more connected to the broader Wii system and its social features. This allowed players to communicate more naturally with friends, adding a level of personal interaction that text chat alone could not achieve. The Mii masks also created a fun, playful way to show off your character’s identity, blending the player’s real-life Mii with the in-game world.
